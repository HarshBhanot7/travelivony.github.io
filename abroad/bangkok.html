Weekend Trip to Bangkok Package


  About the Destination:


Grabbing the interest of tourists from all over the world, Bangkok has been inviting lots of people towards its palm-laden beaches and a plethora of adrenaline rushing activities. This Delhi to Bangkok package will offer you a chance to experience a fun-filled vacation in this capital city of Thailand.


  Weekend Trip To Manali Package Overview


  About the Tour :
  • The first day of Delhi to Bangkok package will commence as you board a flight from Delhi and set foot on the beautiful city of Bangkok. Once you reach here, you will be further taken to your comfy stay where the remaining day will be spent having some leisure time. This day will end by resting overnight.
  • Day two of the Bangkok tour package from Delhi will be a fun-filled one, and today you will be visiting Safari Park and Marine Park. More like a zoo, these places are home to around 100 species of animals resting there peacefully. From watching the dolphins playing in the water to feeding tiger cubs, this place offers a plethora of fun-filled activities one can indulge in. Be it sipping the drinks in the containers shaped like a dolphin or traveling under the sea in Marine Park; this day will bring to you many once in a lifetime experiences
  • The third day of this tour will be dedicated to the Bangkok city, and all the famous places will be covered on this day. Wat Saket, Wat Arun, Chatuchak Market and Grand Palace are some of the most visited places here. At these places, you get to shop for many souvenirs, and if interested in some high-end brands, you can go to Siam Discovery, known to be the best mall.
  • Day four of the tour will be for you to spend however you want. You can either rest in your stay or can travel around the locations on your own. This day will end by reaching back to your visit for the night.
  • Day five will be the last one, and today you will be transferred to the airport for your flight back home.
