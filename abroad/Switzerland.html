Alluring Switzerland Tour Package



  Alluring Switzerland Tour Package Highlight


  • Admire the exceptional beauty of Switzerland, a home to Swiss Alps, fascinating waterfalls, rich culture and amazing cuisines
  • Hear the roar of the magnificent Rhine Falls, the most powerful waterfall in all of Europe and mesmerise the charm that it holds
  • Visit Swiss National Museum and witness the craftsmanship and artworks of Switzerland from its beginnings up to the present day
  • Stroll around the streets of Bahnhofstrasse, one of the world's most expensive and exclusive shopping avenues
  • Enjoy a scenic boat ride to Lake Thun which sits gracefully on the northern periphery of the Alps enveloped in mighty mountains
  • Explore Swiss Open Air Museum that showcases over 100 houses and farm buildings from across the country
  • Enjoy a full day excursion to Mt. Stanserhorn which sits gracefully at an average height of 1850 m and offers panoramic views of Pilatus and Lake Lucerne in the north, and the Bernese Alps in the south

  Alluring Switzerland Tour Package Overview


  About the destination:

Switzerland, a place widely known as the paradise on earth with its mighty mountains, fascinating villages, fancy cities, scenic train rides and so much more. The best way to explore this country's landscape is by train that offers exceptional views. It is a place that must be in everyone's bucket list for the natural beauty of the majestic Alps, the placid lakes and quaint villages can make your experience totally worthwhile. It holds rich culture and art, it is said to be a hub of cultural diversity, inspired by neighbouring countries on all borders. From the buzzing nightlife in Zurich to the picturesque Interlaken, the cities in Switzerland are a mix of culture and beauty.
