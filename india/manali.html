  Weekend Trip To Manali Package Overview


  About the Tour :

With the Dhauladhar and Pir Panjal ranges in the background, the rustic town of Manali offers wildly exotic and spiritually mesmerizing panoramic views. Whether you are a solo wanderer, an adventure enthusiast, or a group traveler, Manali and its mountains will never fail to fascinate you.


Go sliding down the slopes of Solang valley or shop your heart out at the local market, Manali has it all. This weekend tour with Thrillophilia is a perfect option to get a gist of this charming hill station for your much-needed vacation in the mountains.
