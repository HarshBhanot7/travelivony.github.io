  Weekend Trip To Lakshadweep Package Overview


  About the Tour :

Lakshadweep is a preferred holiday destination for a reason - you get the best of everything here. From sightseeing to relaxing at the beaches that offer scenic views, and from water sports to exploring the coral life - you just cannot let go of the islands and their beauty. This package takes you from the hustle-bustle of the city life to serenity mixed with indulgence.


Sightsee the Agatti Island, enjoy local cultural activities at night, walk along the sandy shores here, interact with the natives to learn about the culture, witness amazing coral reefs while enjoying boating, go kayaking, take a dip in the deep-sea waters, experience sports like scuba diving and water skiing, and more. What's the wait for?


Stay

What's better than staying next to the beach amidst luxury? Stay on double/triple sharing basis in spacious rooms, each offering scenic views, comfortable beds and furniture, attached bathroom, and all modern amenities. To ensure utmost hygiene, every room is cleaned and sanitized on a daily basis.


Meals

While a delectable breakfast and dinner are included in the package, you can order lunch at an additional cost. Do not miss out trying the local delicacies and seafood while here


Activities

  • Sightsee the scenic islands of Lakshadweep with boat rides and guided tours
  • Go snorkelling, scuba diving, water skiing, kayaking and more.
  • Explore the breathtaking coral life underwater here
  • Visit the only museum pon Lakshadweep and get to know the culture here
  • Enjoy cultural activities at night at the resort

Transport

Enjoy safe and sanitized inter-island transportation and airport pick-up and drop with this package. The driver will be carrying a Covid negative report with them.
